\section{How the Vulnerability Was Found}

We first decompiled the APK and inspected the manifest, package structure, and
security-relevant classes such as login, registration, and session-handling
components. We then performed a targeted static analysis to locate values
treated as random, codes, tokens, identifiers, or keys.

AI assistance was used to help choose tooling, refine search strategy, and
evaluate whether particular generator APIs or helper methods were appropriate
for security-sensitive use. For each candidate location, we recorded the class,
method, generator API, and the security role of the generated value. This
workflow allowed us to identify at least one weak use of randomness that was
relevant to application security.
